       EXPORTING

            MARC_VRBWK     =  MARC-VRBWK

  IF NOT FLAG1 IS INITIAL.             " Warnung mu# au#erhalb des

    MESSAGE ID SY-MSGID TYPE SY-MSGTY NUMBER SY-MSGNO     "Message  e575

  ENDIF.
